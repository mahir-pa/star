Personalized Zero-Gravity Meditation & Breathing Exercises

  • Mindful Breathing: Inhale deeply for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 4 seconds. Repeat for 5 minutes.
  • Floating Visualization: Close your eyes and visualize floating through a serene cosmic landscape. Breathe deeply and feel the weightlessness.
  • Progressive Muscle Relaxation: Tense and release muscle groups while floating to promote relaxation.
  • Guided Meditation: Follow a calming audio track while using noise-cancelling headphones for deeper immersion.
